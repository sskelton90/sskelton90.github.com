About Steven Skelton

Steven Skelton is a current Computer Science student at The University of Edinburgh in Edinburgh, Scotland.

He has been interested in computing, and technology in general, for as long as he can remember. Whether that was keeping up with the latest technology news or creating technology himself.

For the summer of 2011, he worked at NCR Mobile creating mobile solutions and since then has been certain that a career in creating software is the one for him.

His real passion within technology is creating software that presents complicated data to users in easy to digest ways. At the moment, he is currently working on a few social gaming projects that he hopes to bring to users in the near future.
